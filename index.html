The key to success starts with us

With over 200 years of piano excellence. The Piano Company started in 1820 Japan, starting out as a piano repair shop soon turned to be the largest piano producer in all of Asia by the 1900's
Come with us through a journey of over 200 years to see how The Piano Company retains its status as the best piano manufacturer in the world.

1850

A line up to see Fumiyo Hayasaka in The Piano Company concert hall in 1850. Fumiyo Hayasaka was one of the earliest Japanese composers , and his first piano was gifted to him by his father which he bought from The Piano Company. From there on Mr. Hayasaka would regularly perform in The Piano Company Hall , and would entertain by the dozens.

1900

An early 1900's Yamaha Grand Piano built by Torakusu Yamaha who was a piano tuner for The Piano Company in the late 1800's
Yamaha would soon rise the ranks of the Company and become CEO
Yamaha would now be a household name for the best Piano's in the world

1950-2000

Post World War II The Piano Company has absorbed Yamaha and Steinway & Sons as the dominant Piano Manufacturer in the world. It became the world's largest producers of musical instruments. In 1977 the Yamaha CS-80 was released.

2010

By the 21rst century. The Piano Company is still the biggest piano manufactuer today by a wide margin. It's popular brands such as Yamaha, Sennheiser, and Kawai boasts the highest quality musical instrument in its genre. The Piano Company continues to innovate and expand its horizon through different paths of technology.
